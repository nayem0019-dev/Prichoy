FAQs

Common Questions

What kind of clothing do you sell?▾
Prichoy offers a curated men's collection: ethnic wear, casual essentials, formal pieces, and festive outfits.
How long does delivery take?▾
Inside Dhaka city: within 48 hours. Outside Dhaka: within 72 hours. We deliver nationwide across Bangladesh via trusted courier partners.
What is the return policy?▾
You can check the product in front of the delivery person when it arrives. If not satisfied, return it on the spot — a delivery charge applies. No returns are accepted after the delivery person leaves.
How do the auto-discount coupons work?▾
Selected men's sale items have auto-applied coupons. Add an item to your cart and the discount applies instantly.
Do you accept Cash on Delivery?▾
Yes! We accept Cash on Delivery nationwide for all orders. Online payment (bKash, Nagad, Card) is coming soon.
Can I cancel my order?▾
Orders can be cancelled within 12 hours of placing them. Contact us immediately via WhatsApp at 01762647661.
Do you offer bulk or wholesale orders?▾
Yes. We offer special pricing for 10+ pieces from our men's collection. Email nayem@mail.com for wholesale inquiries.
